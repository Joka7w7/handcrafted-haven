# 🧶 Handcrafted Haven

A virtual marketplace connecting artisans and crafters with customers who appreciate the beauty and quality of handmade products.

## 👥 Team Members

- Jose Chanax

## 📖 Project Overview

Handcrafted Haven is an innovative web application that provides a platform for artisans to showcase and sell their unique handcrafted items. The application focuses on fostering a sense of community, supporting local artisans, and promoting sustainable consumption.

## ✨ Features

- **Seller Profiles** – Authenticated sellers can showcase their craftsmanship, share their story, and display a curated collection of handcrafted items.
- **Product Listings** – Artisans can list items with descriptions, pricing, and images. Buyers can browse and filter by category, price range, and more.
- **Reviews & Ratings** – Any user can leave a star rating and written review on any product.
- **Shopping Cart & Checkout** – Secure e-commerce capabilities for buyers to purchase items.
- **Search** – Find products and artisans by keyword, category, or filter.

## 🎨 Design 

- **Color palette:** Earthy, warm tones — browns, creams, and muted greens that evoke the handmade aesthetic.
- **Typography:** Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body/UI)
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first design, optimized for smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
- **Accessible:** Targets WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance throughout.
